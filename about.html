Our Vision

To be a strong Pentecostal church that meets the needs of the society holistically.

Our Mission

To preach the word of God to all nations in preparation of the second coming of the Lord Jesus Christ, by reaching out and meeting spiritual, economic, and social needs.

Supporting Our Community

FPFK Bethania Academy is deeply committed to social and economic empowerment. Since 1992, we have supported over 400 needy students.

Through our partnership with the Aga Khan Foundation and the Local FPFK Church, we provide school fees and nutritional support to approximately 10% of our student body annually, ensuring quality education for all.
