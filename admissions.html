The Enrollment Process

  1. Pick or download the Admission Form.
  2. Submit form with required documents.
  3. Learner undergoes placement interview.
  4. Payment of admission and tuition fees.
  5. Issuance of school uniform and booklist.

Admission Requirements

  • Original Birth Certificate & 2 Copies.
  • NEMIS Number / UPI Number.
  • KPSEA transition / Assessment reports.
  • 3 Recent Passport-size photographs.
