Welcome to Our School

A Legacy of Excellence

Founded in 1992 by the FPFK Likoni Church Women's Fellowship, Bethania Academy has grown from just nine learners to a leading educational hub in Mombasa County. We are a Christian-based institution committed to holistic development.

Academic Giants

Known for top-tier performance, our Grade 9 learners recently excelled with 75% joining Cluster 1 schools, and our top student scoring an impressive 70/72 points. We remain the best performing school in Likoni and a top-ranked institution in Mombasa County.

Co-Curricular Kings

Beyond academics, we dominate in the arts. We are renowned for our award-winning traditional and modern dance troupes, consistently taking top honors in regional competitions.
